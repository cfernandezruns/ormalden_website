This is the homepage for the chapter of Our Revolution representing Malden, Massachusetts.

We are a municipal political action committee (MuniPAC) that advocates for the election of progressive politicians, and pushes for elected officials to enact progressive policies for our city.

Election Awareness Standout Series

Every Tuesday, 5:30pm - 6:30pm

Malden Center Train Station, Malden, MA 02148

OR Malden is holding a series of Election Outreach events every Tuesday at Malden Center.

We will be getting the word out about this fall's municipal elections, as well as helping people get registered to vote and request mail-in ballots.

Following our endorsement process, these events will also feature us holding signs and engaging with voters in support of our endorsed candidates.

Please feel free to join us! All are welcome!
